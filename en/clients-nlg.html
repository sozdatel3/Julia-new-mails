Your Children. Your Money. Your Peace of Mind: Financial & Legal Protection for Families in the U.S.

During the webinar, we will discuss:

  • how financial and legal planning complement each other and together create a reliable protection system for a family;
  • the basics of life insurance, including Living Benefits for critical, chronic, and terminal illnesses;
  • disability income insurance and where a family can receive funds if a person is temporarily or permanently unable to work;
  • life insurance for children: how it works, when it may make sense, and common misconceptions;
  • the main types of life insurance and how to choose a solution that fits your family;
  • what every family needs to know about guardianship for minor children, wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and estate planning;
  • the most common mistakes in family financial and legal planning and how to avoid them.
