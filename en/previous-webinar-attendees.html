Over the past few years, I have seen that questions about financial and legal protection for a family matter to almost everyone, especially parents. At the same time, many people do not know where to start, which documents are truly necessary, or how to financially protect their family in the event of unexpected life circumstances.

That is why I would like to invite you to a free educational webinar, conducted in Russian. Together with experienced Estate Planning Attorney Natalia Kabbe, we will explain in simple, clear language how to prepare for life's unexpected events and protect your loved ones.

Your Children. Your Money. Your Peace of Mind: Financial & Legal Protection for Families in the U.S.

During the webinar, we will discuss:

  • financial protection for a family in the event of illness, disability, or the loss of a breadwinner;
  • life insurance, Living Benefits, disability income protection, and long-term care programs;
  • guardianship for minor children;
  • wills, trusts, and other estate planning tools;
  • common mistakes in financial and legal family planning and how to avoid them.
